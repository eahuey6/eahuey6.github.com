How to Play BlackJack

Your goal is to get a higher score than the dealer without going over 21. If at any point your score goes over 21, you automatically lose. If your score is 21 exactly, you automatically win.

At the beginning of the game, you will recieve 2 cards. Your score will be the added values of the cards in your hand.

Cards have the same values as their numbers but this game, the value of an Ace is one. The dealer will draw one card at the start.

If you want the dealer to deal you another card, press the hit button. But if you do not want another card, press the stand button and the dealer will play out their turn.
