Space Email

Space Email is also somewhat similar to my project in that it deals with anonymous messages. This website allows the user to receive and send anonymous emails to random people. The design of this website fits the space theme but the interface is also pretty limited. Some of the buttons and actions are not intuitive though. In order to receive an anonymous email, the user must click the refresh button, which is a bit unclear. Some of the other buttons are also unclear as to what they lead to and their purpose. Overall its a simple design and concept but the user experience could be improved.
